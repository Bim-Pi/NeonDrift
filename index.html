NEON DRIFT

Dodge hazards, collect energy, and keep your combo chain alive.

Controls

  • ←→ or AD to drift
  • Space to boost (uses energy)
  • P pause • R restart

Mobile: drag left/right • tap with 2 fingers to boost

How scoring works

  • Energy orbs add score and fill your boost meter
  • A clean streak increases Chain multiplier
  • Getting hit resets the chain and drains some score

Tip: short boosts help you thread tight gaps.

Goal

Survive as long as possible. The track speeds up, hazards get trickier, and your combo chain becomes everything.

Powerups

  • Shield (cyan): blocks one hit
  • Magnet (purple): pulls nearby orbs for a short time
  • Slow-mo (green): slows hazards briefly (score still counts!)

Accessibility

Press H anytime to toggle the help panel, and M to mute.
